Tic Tac Toe Game
Welcome to the Tic Tac Toe game! This simple yet classic game is perfect for passing the time and challenging your friends.

How to Play
Objective
The objective of Tic Tac Toe is to be the first player to get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal on the game board.

Game Rules
The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
Player X always goes first, followed by player O.
Players take turns placing their symbol (X or O) on an empty cell of the grid.
The game continues until one player achieves a winning combination or the board is filled (resulting in a draw).
A winning combination consists of three of the same symbols (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal.
If a winning combination is achieved, the game ends, and the winning player is declared.
If the board is filled without any player achieving a winning combination, the game ends in a draw.
Tutorial
Starting a Game: To start a new game, simply run the Tic Tac Toe program. Player X will always go first.
Making a Move: When it's your turn, input the row and column numbers where you want to place your symbol. For example, "1 2" would place your symbol in the first row, second column.
Winning the Game: Achieve a winning combination of three of your symbols in a row, column, or diagonal to win the game.
Ending the Game: After each game, you will be prompted to continue playing or leave the game. Enter "yes" to start a new game or "no" to exit.
Additional Features
Statistics and Leaderboards: Track your win-loss records and compete for the top spot on the leaderboards.
Customization Options: Customize game settings such as board size, symbols, and game mode.
Multiplayer Support: Challenge your friends to a game of Tic Tac Toe and compete in real-time matches.
